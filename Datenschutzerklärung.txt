Privacy Policy for VaultFlow+                                                                               
                                                                                                              
  Last updated: April 18, 2026
                                                                                                              
  Data Collection
  VaultFlow+ does not collect personal data. All financial data is stored exclusively on your device.         
                  
  AI Features
  VaultFlow+ includes an AI assistant for budget suggestions and financial tips. When using AI features,
  anonymized financial data (categories and amounts only, no personal information) is sent to Anthropic's     
  Claude API for processing. This data is not stored permanently. For more information, see Anthropic's
  Privacy Policy at https://www.anthropic.com/privacy.                                                        
                  
  Third-Party Services                                                                                        
  VaultFlow+ uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data
  to serve personalized ads. See Google's Privacy Policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy.              
                  
  Data Storage                                                                                                
  All app data is stored locally on your device. We have no access to your personal financial data.

  Changes to This Policy
  We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
